About Me

I am an educational researcher studying how artificial intelligence and emerging technologies can support teaching, professional learning, career development, and workforce education. I hold a Joint Ph.D. in Education and Educational Administration from Technische Universität Dresden and Shiraz University, an M.A. in Human Resources Training and Development from Shahid Beheshti University, and a B.A. in Educational Administration and Planning from Shiraz University.

Research Interests
